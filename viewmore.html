You've come to this page to read more about life lessons. I recently finished Mark Manson's book THE SUBTLE ART OF NOT GIVING A FUCK. Because it's the only book I'm reading right now, it's my current favorite. The book taught me valuable life lessons.

Here are my top three favorite Michael Renney lessons that I'd like to share with you:

1.) And that your fear of failure is your biggest enemy.
"We can be truly successful only at something we're willing to fail at. If we're unwilling to fail, then we're unwilling to succeed." 2.) Not every "problem" has to be a negative thing.
"Don't hope for a life without problems. There's no such thing. Instead, hope for a life full of good problems." 3.) Hard times area a necessary part of life.
"Who you are is defined by what you're willing to struggle for."

We are all strong enough to tackle challenges, but we are also vulnerable. We must also be gentle to ourselves.

"Most days we have to remind ourselves to keep it gentle --- no matter how slow we go or no matter the amount of progress we make. It matters when we make a difference, but it matters most if we take good care of ourselves in all aspects. See how things unfold spontaneously --- walk, but not in haste, keep it gentle."
---Jaeka
